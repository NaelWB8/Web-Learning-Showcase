About Me

Hi there! My name is Nathanael Wilson Bong, but most people just call me Nael (or Bong, if they’re feeling bold). I’m a science-stream student with a strong interest in Math, Physics, and Computer Science—and because I like challenges, I also study Economics and Accounting on the side.

When I started this project, I was an 11th-grade student at Kolese Kanisius, right in the heart of Jakarta. My daily routine might look like any other student’s—waking up, going to school, and doing assignments—but I try to make each day a bit more meaningful. When I’m not wrestling with equations, you’ll probably find me coding, working on creative projects, or playing the piano. I love turning ideas into something tangible—whether it’s a small app, a digital design, or a melody that keeps playing in my head.

This website is part of my ongoing journey to combine learning and creativity. It features a drop-down menu with the subjects I study—Mathematics, History, Physics, Indonesian, and Economics—along with materials and notes I’ve prepared myself. Hopefully, you’ll find something useful (or at least interesting) here.

Thanks for visiting—stay curious, stay happy, and Ad Majorem Dei Gloriam!
